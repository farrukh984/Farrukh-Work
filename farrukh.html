Farrukh

Farrukh is a 23-year-old Iranian-American author, known for his short stories and poetry. He has written over 1,000 books, including 38 novels and 67 short stories. Farrukh's work has been translated into 30 languages and is available in various formats, including paperback, hardcover, and e-books. Farrukh is also a member of the Iranian Orthodox Church and has served as a spokesperson for the Christian faith in Iran.
